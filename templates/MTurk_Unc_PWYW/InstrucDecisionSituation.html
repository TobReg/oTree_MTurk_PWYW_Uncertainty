Background

The seller's product can be sold once to each of the three buyers. Each buyer values the product differently; the valuation (what it is worth to a buyer) lies between 2 and 10. The seller's production costs are 3.

Decision situation

First, the seller chooses how to offer the product: "Fixed price" or "pay-what-you-want (PWYW)".

When choosing fixed price the seller will set a price. Then, each buyer decides whether to buy at that price or not.

  • If a buyer decides against the purchase, there will be no transaction: 0 for this buyer, 0 for the seller (from that buyer)
  • If a buyer decides to purchase, the seller receives the set price and the buyer gets their valuation minus the price.

If the seller chooses PWYW, the payments are determined by the buyers. Each buyer decides how much to pay:

  • The seller receives the payment of each buyer.
  • Each buyer gets their valuation minus the payment they made.

Payoffs

The payoff for the seller is the sum of the buyers' payments minus 3 (production costs).

The payoff for a buyer is the valuation minus the payment; or 0 in case the buyer decided not to buy.

The seller learns about the payments made. The buyers are not informed about the payments made by other buyers. The experiment ends after one round.

Information about valuations

{% if treatment == 1 %}

The seller knows the valuations of the three buyers. The buyers know their respective valuations, but they do not know valuations of other buyers.

{% elif treatment == 2 %}

The seller knows the valuations of the three buyers. The buyers do not know their valuation, neither the valuation of other buyers'.

In case the seller selects PWYW: If the buyer decides to purchase, the buyer will be informed about their valuation before making the payment decision.

{% elif treatment == 3 %}

The seller does not know the valuations of the three buyers. The buyers know their respective valuations, but they do not know valuations of other buyers.

{% elif treatment == 4 %}

The valuations of the buyers are not known, neither to the seller nor to the buyers.

In case the seller selects PWYW: If the buyer decides to purchase, the buyer will be informed about their valuation before making the payment decision.

{% endif %}
