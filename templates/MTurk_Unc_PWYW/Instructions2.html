Instructions

In this experiment you can earn a bonus depending on your decisions and the decisions of other participants. It is therefore important that you read the instructions carefully and completely.

Procedures

The experiment will last about 15 minutes. Payoffs are shown in points; 1 point = {{ session.real_world_currency_per_point }} USD.

Overview

Four participants interact with each other, one seller and three buyers. Your role will be randomly assigned at the beginning of the experiment. It is very important that you familiarize yourself with both roles.

Participants

The other participants in this experiment are MTurkers like you. All participants remain anonymous. Neither during nor after the experiment you will get to know with whom you were connected and vice versa.

Earnings

Your earnings consist of the participation fee ({{ session.participation_fee }}) plus the bonus (base and payoff). If you quit the experiment before the end, you will not receive any earnings! If you quit, you will also keep three other MTurkers from completing the experiment.
