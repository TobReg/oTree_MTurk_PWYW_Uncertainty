Instructions

In this experiment you can earn money depending on your decisions and the decisions of other participants. It is therefore important that you read the instructions carefully and completely.

Procedures

The experiment will last about 15 minutes. Payoffs are shown in ECU (Experimental Currency Units); 1 ECU = 0.1 USD.

Overview

Four participants interact with each other, one seller and three buyers. Your role will be randomly assigned at the beginning of the experiment. It is very important that you familiarize yourself with both roles.

Participants

The other participants in this experiment are MTurkers like you. All participants remain anonymous. Neither during nor after the experiment you will get to know with whom you were connected and vice versa.
